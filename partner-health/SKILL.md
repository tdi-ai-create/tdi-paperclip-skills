## Purpose

## The scoring model — 100 points across five signals

Every active partner receives a 0-100 health score calculated from five signals. The score determines the partner's tier and the appropriate response.

**Signal 1: Delivery completion — 30 points**
Are contracted sessions being delivered on schedule?
- 30 pts: All sessions on track or ahead of schedule
- 20 pts: Minor delay, recovery plan in place
- 10 pts: Significantly behind, no clear recovery plan
- 0 pts: Stalled — no sessions delivered in 30+ days
- Note: Hub-only partnerships (D41) are neutral on this signal — no sessions are contracted

**Signal 2: Collections health — 25 points**
Are invoices current?
- 25 pts: All invoices paid, nothing overdue
- 15 pts: Minor delay, payment in progress
- 5 pts: Overdue invoice unresolved beyond 30 days
- 0 pts: Active collections dispute or non-payment

**Signal 3: Renewal timeline — 20 points**
How far out is the renewal and is a conversation underway?
- 20 pts: 90+ days out, no urgency
- 15 pts: 60-90 days, renewal conversation starting
- 8 pts: 30-60 days, active renewal risk, no proposal sent
- 0 pts: Under 30 days, no active proposal — critical

**Signal 4: Next session scheduled — 15 points**
Is the next service session on the calendar?
- 15 pts: Next session confirmed
- 8 pts: Session overdue, needs scheduling
- 0 pts: No session scheduled, contract live
- Note: Hub-only partnerships are neutral (15 pts default)

**Signal 5: Task backlog — 10 points**
Are TDI-side tasks current?
- 10 pts: No overdue tasks
- 6 pts: 1-2 overdue tasks
- 2 pts: 3+ overdue tasks
- 0 pts: Significant backlog, relationship momentum at risk

---

## The four tiers

**Strong — 75 to 100**
Partnership is healthy. No immediate action required. Monitor passively. Surfaces in weekly analytics summary only.

**Watch — 50 to 74**
One or more signals showing strain. Priya reviews within 48 hours and determines if proactive outreach is needed. Does not automatically go to Rae unless Priya flags it.

**At Risk — 25 to 49**
Meaningful renewal risk. Routes to Priya's task queue as urgent. Also surfaces on Rae's dashboard. Priya acts within 48 hours. Rae is notified same day.

**Critical — 0 to 24**
Likely churn without immediate intervention. Routes to Rae's approval queue immediately alongside Priya's queue. Rae and Priya coordinate response together.

## Hub engagement signals — second layer

For partners actively using the Learning Hub, these additional signals layer onto the base score:

- Hub engagement below 50% of contract average: +2 risk points
- Mood average declining for 3+ consecutive weeks: +2 risk points
- School champion activity has dropped significantly: +2 risk points
- No Hub logins in 14 days: +3 risk points (highest Hub signal)
- Quick wins below 1 per user per month: +1 risk point

Hub composite score:
- 0-2: Green — healthy Hub engagement
- 3-5: Amber — watch
- 6+: Red — Hub engagement at risk, flag to Priya

## Partner personality types

**Self-serve partners (e.g. Glen Ellyn D41)**
Low responsiveness is their style, not a risk signal. D41 has 6 of 10 seats active across 20 courses — that's strong engagement. They bought 10 seats to have extras. Dee Neukirch rarely responds but the partnership is healthy. For self-serve partners, engagement data matters more than responsiveness.

Flag: If Hub engagement drops significantly for a self-serve partner, that IS a signal — they're disengaging quietly.

**Champion-dependent partners**
Everything runs through one person. When that person goes quiet — doesn't log in, stops messaging, misses meetings — the whole partnership is at risk. Flag champion inactivity within 14 days for these partners.

**Vocal partners**
Always have a concern, always renew. High-maintenance but low churn risk. Don't let their volume signal distract from partners who are quietly at risk.

## Priya's operational playbook

**When a partner moves to Watch:**
Review the specific signal that dropped the score. If it's delivery-related, check the session schedule and flag to James. If it's collections, flag to Sandra immediately. If it's renewal timing, draft an outreach message for Rae's approval. Do not let a Watch partner sit more than 48 hours without a decision on whether to act.

**When a partner moves to At Risk:**
Contact the partner within 48 hours. Do not wait for Rae's calendar — initiate the outreach and loop Rae in with full context before she speaks to anyone at the school. Brief includes: current score, which signals are low, what was said in last contact, recommended next step.

**When a partner moves to Critical:**
This is a same-day call to Rae. Not a Slack message, not an email — a call. Priya and Rae decide the response together. The partner does not get any communication from TDI until Rae approves the approach.
