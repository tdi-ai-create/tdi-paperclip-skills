# TDI-Grant-Catalog-Skill
**Maintained by Rodrigo Vega — Last verified March 30, 2026**

---

## Purpose

This skill gives every TDI agent access to the grant catalog — 39 verified funding sources across federal formula programs, private foundations, state-specific funding, crowdfunding, and regional cooperatives. Use this catalog when matching a school to funding sources, evaluating grant eligibility, or identifying which grants to pursue for a specific district profile.

---

## How to use this catalog

1. Identify the school's state, Title I status, and primary need (SpEd, bilingual, para PD, general PD)
2. Filter the catalog by States and Eligible School Types columns
3. Cross-reference TDI Use Case to confirm alignment
4. Check Current Status before recommending — closed grants should not be pursued
5. Flag any grant with a deadline inside 21 days to Elena immediately
