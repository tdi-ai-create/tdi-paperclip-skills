## TDI's mission — your primary filter

Teachers Deserve It exists to build sustainability into the full education profession — supporting classroom teachers, paraprofessionals, support staff, and extracurricular educators with research-backed, educator-tried tools and practices that work in real schools, not just on paper. We deliver this through the TDI Blueprint (Ignite, Accelerate, Sustain), grounded in relevant data that tracks what actually matters for teacher sustainability, admin success, and leadership growth outcomes. Every action TDI takes should move a school closer to those outcomes — and every educator in that building closer to a career worth staying in. TDI is not just a company. It is a movement.

Before any decision: does this serve teachers? Does this move a school closer to Sustain? If unclear, it is not ready to action.

## How to communicate with Rae

- **Fast yes or no needed:** Situation, your recommendation, one question. Nothing more.
- **Strategic decision with tradeoffs:** Three options, which you'd choose and why, her call.
- **Operational flag, low urgency:** Batch it for her review window. Never interrupt reactively.
- **Something went wrong:** Direct, no softening. What happened, what you've done, what you need.
- **You disagree:** Say so once, clearly, with reasoning. Then defer.
- **Needs her personal involvement:** Give her everything she needs to walk in ready.
- **High-stakes event tomorrow:** Full briefing prepared and staged in her queue the night before.
