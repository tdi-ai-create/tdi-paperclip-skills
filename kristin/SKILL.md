## MONDAY POST FORMULA — FREE

**Purpose:** Awareness + Community + Starter Value. Shareable. Reader leaves thinking "Someone finally said this."

**Structure:**
- Opening (first 2 sentences): Reader knows exactly what they're getting and why it matters. No slow build-up.
- The rant: Name the systemic problem clearly. Make it specific enough that teachers see themselves. Never shame — always validate. "This isn't a you problem. It's a system problem."
- ONE concrete starter idea: Not a full strategy — just enough to feel like something changed today. Can be a sentence stem, script, or reframe.
- Closing engagement question: Storytelling format. Makes readers want to share their own story. Drives comments and shares.
- Wednesday tease (preferred): One sentence cliffhanger. "Wednesday I'm going deeper on exactly how to..."
- TDI Facebook Group link: https://www.facebook.com/groups/tdimovement

---

## WEDNESDAY POST FORMULA — PAID (partial)

**Purpose:** Deep Dive + True Solutions. Reader leaves thinking "I know exactly what to do now."

**Structure:**
- 3-4 strategies with real classroom examples
- Specific scripts teachers can use
- K-12 appeal — works for new and veteran teachers
- Paywall placement: determined AFTER drafting, not predetermined. Gate goes right before templates, planners, or downloadable resource.
- Vault resource: walk readers up to it, show what it does, make them picture using it tomorrow morning. Link takes them to the paid gate — no "paid subscribers get..." language. Value leads, gate follows naturally.
- TDI Facebook Group link: https://www.facebook.com/groups/tdimovement

## EDITORIAL NON-NEGOTIABLES

- No em dashes — ever. Use commas, periods, or "and"
- No bullet points in articles — flowing prose only
- No business jargon (ROI, stakeholders, conversion rate, etc.)
- No slow build-up openings — value in first two sentences
- No repeating similar themes week over week
- No profanity (not even with ***)
- No announcing the paywall — value leads, gate follows naturally
- Light witty asides encouraged: "oof," "eek," "yikes"
- Voice: "we/our team" for TDI work, "I" only for personal story hooks
- Content must feel timely and forward-looking
- Always link TDI Facebook Group: https://www.facebook.com/groups/tdimovement
- Deliver as .md files
