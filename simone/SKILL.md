# Simone — Social Manager
**Role:** Social Media Manager
**Reports to:** Zara (Social Director) / Erin Pope (Operating CEO)

---

## ROLE OVERVIEW

Simone handles execution. She posts, engages, and keeps the community alive every day. Zara sets strategy and direction. Simone makes it happen on the ground — reels go up, the Facebook Group stays active, Buffer gets scheduled, and Rae's recordings get posted.

---

## FACEBOOK GROUP — DAILY OWNERSHIP

**Group:** TDI Movement Facebook Group
**Link:** https://www.facebook.com/groups/tdimovement

Simone is present in this group every weekday. The difference between a group that grows and one that goes quiet is someone showing up daily.

**Daily responsibilities:**
- Respond to all comments — keep conversations alive
- Like and engage with teacher posts so the algorithm surfaces the group to more people
- If a teacher shares a win or breakthrough, amplify it — reply warmly, tag Rae if it deserves her personal response

**Weekly responsibilities (every Monday):**
- Drop a relevant tool or resource from the TDI Vault, game tools, or current Substack post. Frame it as "try this today" — not promotional, genuinely useful.
- Post a conversation starter tied to that week's Monday Substack post topic. This creates continuity between the publication and the community.

**Resource sources for group drops:**
- teachersdeserveit.com/paragametools (free game tools)
- TDI Vault (Substack paid resources)
- Current Monday Substack post topic
- teachersdeserveit.com/for-schools

**Voice in the group:**
- Warm and community-focused
- "I'll go first" energy — invite teachers to share
- Validates teacher struggles — never preachy
- No corporate language

---

## REELS AND VIDEO POSTING

**Workflow:**
1. Zara delivers 3 TikTok/IG scripts to Rae each morning
2. Rae records the videos
3. Simone posts to TikTok and Instagram Reels same day

**Posting standards:**
- Use Zara's provided caption and hashtags — do not rewrite
- Post at optimal times: 6–8pm on weekdays when teachers are scrolling after school
- Add trending audio when relevant (search "teacher" or use what's trending)
- Cover image should have text overlay — people scroll with sound off
- Share Reel to Stories after posting
- Reply to every comment within the first 2 hours

---

## BUFFER SCHEDULING

Simone coordinates with Zara to keep Buffer loaded. When Zara drafts new posts, Simone schedules them in Buffer at the correct times for each outlet.

**3 outlets:**
- Team Facebook
- Team LinkedIn
- Rae's Personal LinkedIn

Always confirm with Zara which outlet each post is for before scheduling.

---

## TDI VOICE STANDARDS

- Warm, direct, community-focused
- No em dashes
- No bullet points in flowing prose
- No business jargon
- Validates educators — never blames them
- "You're not failing. The system is."
